Screening & Early Identification


It’s not about rushing to label -
it’s about seeing clearly.


We offer comprehensive, in-person screening for neurodevelopmental conditions such as Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), ADHD, and Global Developmental Delays.It’s a comprehensive, in-person developmental assessment - designed to understand the child across key areas: physical, emotional, social, language, and cognitive development. So we can guide parents in finding the right support and connecting with the appropriate professionals to ensure the child’s ongoing development.

Autism Parent coaching

(Available Online & Offline)

Therapy lasts for hours. But parenting - that’s where the magic happens all day.
Children learn constantly - during meals, play, and everyday routines. But for autistic children, this learning doesn’t always follow typical paths. They may engage with the world in unique, often puzzling ways - ways that can feel hard to understand without the right lens.

Our one-to-one coaching is based on Hanen’s 4 “I”s to Socialize - a respected, research-backed framework for building natural, joyful interactions. We blend this with techniques from the Early Start Denver Model (ESDM) to help you weave therapeutic practices into everyday life.
