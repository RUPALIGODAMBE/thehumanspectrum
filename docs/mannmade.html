MannMade

MannMade is an initiative that creates real and respectful earning opportunities for persons with disabilities by meeting them where they are. It is not about making people fit into predefined standards. It is about creating a dignified path to earning through who they already are and what they already express.
In our expressive arts sessions, individuals with neurodivergence create artwork that is full of emotion, spontaneity, and honesty. These are not just drawings or paintings. They are stories. They are windows into a world that is often unseen.
Through MannMade, we are working towards transforming these artworks into premium, fashion-forward merchandise, starting with high-quality, limited-edition t-shirts.
A portion of the profits from every sale will go directly to the artist, making this not only a creative collaboration but a source of income and recognition. It is a way for them to participate in the economy, to earn through their unique strengths, and to feel the pride of being seen, valued, and paid for their contribution.

This is not charity.
This is not tokenism.
